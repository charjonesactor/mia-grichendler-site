Mia Grichendler

Bio

Mia Grichendler is a French-American actress based in New York City. She obtained a degree in neuroscience which led to a near three-year stint working on Wall Street, before finally deciding to follow her artistic passions and pursue a career in theater.

Ballet has always been a major component of her life, having attended pre-professional ballet schools at Houston Ballet, SAB, and UNCSA.
